How many integrals are needed to express this area as an integral or sum of integrals with respect to $x$? $\answer{1}$

How many integrals are needed to express this area as an integral or sum of integrals with respect to $y$? $\answer{3}$

Set up, but do not evaluate, an integral with respect to $x$ that would give the area of the region.

\[
A= \int_{x=\answer{(5-\sqrt{15})/2}}^{x=\answer{(5+\sqrt{15})/2}} \answer{(-x^2+4x)-(x^2-6x+5)  } \d x
\]

\begin{hint}
To find the limits of integration, set $-x^2+4x=x^2-6x+5$.  
\begin{align*}
 -x^2+4x &=x^2-6x+5 \\ 
 \answer{-2x^2+10x-5} &= 0
\end{align*}
Now use the quadratic formula to solve for $x$.
\end{hint}
