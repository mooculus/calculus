\begin{exercise}
Let $f(x) = \frac{ 2 e^x-x^5}{1-3 x^5}$. Compute:
\[
f'(x)
\begin{prompt}=\answer{\frac{(2 e^x-5{x}^{4})(1-3x^5)-(2 e^x-x^5)(-15x^4)}{(1-3x^5)^{2}}}\end{prompt}
\]
\end{exercise}
