\begin{exercise}

Let $f$ be given by
\[
f(x) = 
\begin{cases}
4-x^2 & \text{if } x>0\\
4-\frac{x^2}{4} & \text{if } x\leq 0
\end{cases}.
\]

The figure shows a rectangle with its sides parallel to the coordinate axes with two of its vertices on the curve $y=f(x)$ and \textbf{above} the $x$-axis and the other two vertices on the $x$-axis.  Let the bottom right vertex be at the point $\left(x,0\right)$ for $0<x<2$.

\begin{image}
  \begin{tikzpicture}
    \begin{axis}[
        xmin=-4.3,xmax=2.3,ymin=-0.3,ymax=4.8,
        clip=false,
        unit vector ratio*=1 1 1,
        axis lines=center,
        grid = major,
        ytick={0,1,...,4},
	xtick={-4,-3,...,2},
        xlabel=$x$, ylabel=$y$,
        every axis y label/.style={at=(current axis.above origin),anchor=south},
        every axis x label/.style={at=(current axis.right of origin),anchor=west},
      ]
      \addplot[very thick,penColor,domain=-4:0] plot{4-x^2/4};
      \addplot[very thick,penColor,domain=0:2] plot{4-x^2};

      \draw[red,fill,very thick,opacity=0.2] 
      (axis cs:1.2, 0) -- (axis cs:1.2, 4-1.2^2) -- (axis cs:-2*1.2,4-1.2^2) -- (axis cs:-2*1.2,0) -- cycle;
      \addplot[red,fill=red,only marks,mark=*,scale=0.2] coordinates{(1.2,0)};
      
      \node[penColor] at (axis cs:2, 3.5) {$y=4-x^2$};
      \node[penColor] at (axis cs:-3, 3.5) {$y=4-\frac{x^2}{4}$};
      \node[penColor] at (axis cs:1.4,-.2) {$x$};
      \end{axis}`
  \end{tikzpicture}
\end{image}

The area of this rectangle as a function of $x$ is 
\[
A(x) = \answer{12x - 3x^3}
\]

\end{exercise}
