\begin{exercise}
Consider the power series $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{k^2 }{10^k (k+5)}(x+7)^{k+3}$.

The radius of convergence for the power series is $\answer{10}$.

The open interval of convergence is $\left(\answer{-17},\answer{3}\right)$.

\end{exercise}
