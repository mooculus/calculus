\begin{exercise}
Let $\vecl_{1}(t)$ be the line whose components are $\left\{ \begin{aligned}
x(t) & =0.1+1.1t\\
y(t) & =2.9-1.5t\\
z(t) & =3.2+1.6t
\end{aligned}
\right.$  and $\vecl_{2}(t)$ be the line whose components are $\left\{ \begin{aligned}
x(t) & =4-2.1t\\
y(t) & =1.8+7.2t\\
z(t) & =3.1+1.1t
\end{aligned}
\right.$

Then $\vecl_{1}$ and $\vecl_{2}$ are: 

\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice{parallel}
\choice{intersecting}
\choice[correct]{non-intersecting}
\choice{the same}
\end{multipleChoice}


\end{exercise}
