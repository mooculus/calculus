\begin{exercise}
Let $\vecl_{1}(t)=\vector{3,4,1}+t\vector{2,-3,4}$ and $\vecl_{2}(t)=\vector{-3,3,-3}+t\vector{3,-2,4}$. 

Then $\vecl_{1}$ and $\vecl_{2}$ are: 
\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice{parallel}
\choice[correct]{intersecting}
\choice{non-intersecting}
\choice{the same}
\end{multipleChoice}

\end{exercise}
