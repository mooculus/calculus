\begin{exercise}

The angle of elevation of the sun is $22^{\circ}$.  A 6ft tall man is standing in the shadow of a 76ft tall tree so that the tips of their shadows coincide.  Sketch the situation.  Find the distance between the man and the tree, rounded to the nearest foot.

STEP 1
Introduce a variable $x$ that denotes the distance between the man and the tree. Then sketch and label the figure.

\begin{hint}

STEP 2
Express the angle of elevation in  radians
\[
22^{\circ}=22\cdot\answer{\frac{\pi}{180}}\text{ rad}.
\]
STEP 3
Introduce a new variable, $s$, that denotes the length of the man's shadow. Compute $s$ using the angle in radians found in Step 2:

      \draw[thick] (D)--(E)--(C)--cycle;
           \draw[thick] (D)--(E)--(C)--cycle;

      \node at (4.27,1.6) {$x$};
       \node at (1.27,1.6) {$s$};
       \node at (1.32,2.3) {\tiny$\frac{11\pi}{90}$};
     % \node at (2.6,3) {$6$};

\[
s=\frac{6}{\tan{\left(\answer{\frac{11\pi}{90}}\right)}}
\]
STEP 4

The figure shows two similar right triangles, so it follows that
\[
\frac{s}{6}=\frac{\answer{s+x}}{76}
\]

STEP 5

Solve for $x$,  rounded to the nearest foot.
\[
x=\answer{173}ft.
\]
