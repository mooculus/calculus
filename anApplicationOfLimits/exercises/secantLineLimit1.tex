Assume that the function $s(t) = t^3 - 2t^2$ represents the position of an object at time $t$ moving along a line.  The average velocity of the object over the interval of time $\left[1,2\right]$ is $\answer{1}$.

\begin{exercise}

\[
\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|}

Average velocity & \answer{1} & \answer{2.25} & \answer{3.61} & \answer{4.41} & \answer{4.04} \\\hline
\end{array}
\]
